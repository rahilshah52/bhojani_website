About the Diabetes & Hypertension Clinic

Diabetes & Hypertension Clinic (DHC) is a specialist outpatient service dedicated to comprehensive management of diabetes mellitus, hypertension and related metabolic conditions. Our multidisciplinary team delivers evidence-based medical care combined with patient education, behavioural support and remote monitoring to achieve sustained improvements in health and quality of life.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, compassionate and affordable care that empowers people living with chronic metabolic disease to lead full, healthy lives. We emphasise early specialist involvement, structured education and continuous support—using the latest clinical techniques and remote monitoring technologies where appropriate.

What we offer

  • Individualised treatment plans for diabetes and hypertension
  • Insulin and medication optimisation, including use of continuous glucose monitoring where indicated
  • Remote vitals monitoring and telehealth consultations
  • Structured education, lifestyle and nutrition support
  • Multidisciplinary coordination with dietitians, nurses and allied health professionals

How we work

We combine specialist clinical assessment with personalised goal-setting and regular remote review. Our approach blends medication management, behaviour change support and technology-assisted monitoring to reduce complications and improve day-to-day wellbeing.

Our approach

  • Personalized care plans tailored to your goals
  • Remote vitals tracking and telehealth visits
  • Medication optimisation and lifestyle coaching

Dr. Asif Bhojani — Consultant Endocrinologist

Dr. Asif Bhojani is a consultant endocrinologist and diabetologist with over 15 years of clinical experience. He specialises in comprehensive diabetes care, hypertension management and metabolic medicine, with particular expertise in insulin therapy optimisation, continuous glucose monitoring and remote patient management.

Professional qualifications
  • MBBS
  • MD in Endocrinology
  • Fellowship training in diabetes and metabolic medicine
Clinical focus and approach

Dr. Bhojani adopts an evidence-based, patient-centred approach. He emphasises personalised goals, medication rationalisation and practical lifestyle strategies. He integrates technology—such as home monitoring and telehealth—to maintain close follow-up and support long-term risk reduction.

Teaching & research

Dr. Bhojani contributes to continuing professional education and has authored peer-reviewed articles on diabetes care. Details of publications and speaking engagements are available on request.
